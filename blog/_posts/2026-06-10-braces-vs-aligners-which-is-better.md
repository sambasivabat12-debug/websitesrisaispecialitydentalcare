## Why Straighten Your Teeth?

Straight teeth are not just about looks. Crooked or crowded teeth:

- Trap food and plaque, causing **cavities and gum disease**
- Create **uneven bite forces** that wear down teeth
- Can cause **jaw pain and headaches**
- Affect speech and chewing efficiency
- Impact confidence in social and professional life

## Traditional Braces

Metal or ceramic brackets bonded to teeth, connected by wires that gradually move teeth into position.

**Advantages:**
- Works for even the most complex cases
- More affordable
- No discipline needed — they work 24/7 since you can't remove them

**Considerations:**
- Visible (ceramic braces are less noticeable)
- Food restrictions — no hard or sticky foods
- Requires careful brushing around brackets

## Clear Aligners

A series of transparent, removable trays custom-made for your teeth. Each set moves teeth slightly; you change trays every 1–2 weeks.

**Advantages:**
- Nearly **invisible** — most people won't notice
- **Removable** — eat anything, brush normally
- More comfortable, no wires poking
- Fewer clinic visits

**Considerations:**
- Must be worn **20–22 hours a day** — discipline required
- More expensive than metal braces
- Not suitable for very complex corrections

## Treatment Time Comparison

Both typically take **12 to 24 months** depending on case complexity. Mild crowding may finish in as little as 6–8 months with aligners.

## Cost in Hyderabad

- **Metal braces:** ₹25,000 – ₹45,000
- **Ceramic braces:** ₹35,000 – ₹55,000
- **Clear aligners:** ₹60,000 – ₹1,50,000 depending on case

## Which Should You Choose?

Choose **braces** if you have complex corrections, a tighter budget, or worry about wearing trays consistently. Choose **aligners** if appearance during treatment matters, you want food freedom, and your case is mild to moderate.

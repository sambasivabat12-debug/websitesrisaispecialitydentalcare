## What is a Dental Crown?

A dental crown (commonly called a "cap") is a custom-made cover that fits over a damaged, weakened, or root canal treated tooth — restoring its shape, strength, and appearance.

## When Do You Need a Crown?

- After **root canal treatment** — the tooth becomes brittle and needs protection
- A tooth with a **large filling** and little natural structure left
- A **cracked or fractured** tooth
- A severely **worn down or discoloured** tooth
- On top of a **dental implant**
- To anchor a **dental bridge**

## Types of Crowns Compared

**Metal crowns** — strong but unaesthetic silver colour. Rarely chosen today.

**PFM (Porcelain Fused to Metal)** — tooth-coloured outside, metal inside. Affordable, but a grey line can appear at the gum over time.

**Zirconium crowns** — the modern gold standard. Made from zirconia, a biocompatible ceramic that is:

- **Extremely strong** — withstands full chewing forces, even on molars
- **Completely metal-free** — no grey gum line, no metal allergies
- **Naturally translucent** — matches your real teeth perfectly
- **Long-lasting** — 15+ years with good care

## The Crown Procedure

1. **Tooth preparation** — the tooth is shaped under local anaesthesia
2. **Digital impression** — we capture the exact shape of your tooth
3. **Temporary crown** placed while your permanent crown is made
4. **Fitting appointment** — usually within 3–5 days, your zirconium crown is checked for fit, bite, and colour, then permanently cemented

## Crown Cost in Hyderabad

- **PFM crown:** ₹3,500 – ₹6,000
- **Zirconium crown:** ₹8,000 – ₹15,000 depending on the grade

A zirconium crown costs more upfront but its strength, appearance, and longevity make it the best value over time.

## How to Care for Your Crown

Treat it like a natural tooth — brush twice daily, floss around it, avoid chewing very hard items like ice, and visit us every 6 months for a check-up.

## What is a Smile Makeover?

A smile makeover is a personalised combination of cosmetic dental treatments designed to give you your ideal smile. It addresses everything you'd like to change — colour, shape, alignment, gaps, and proportions — in one coordinated plan.

## What Can a Smile Makeover Fix?

- **Stained or yellowed teeth** → professional whitening
- **Chipped, worn, or uneven teeth** → veneers or bonding
- **Gaps between teeth** → veneers, bonding, or aligners
- **Crooked or crowded teeth** → braces or clear aligners
- **Gummy smile** → gum contouring
- **Missing teeth** → implants or bridges
- **Old discoloured fillings** → tooth-coloured replacements

## Popular Smile Makeover Treatments

**Veneers** — ultra-thin porcelain shells bonded to the front of teeth. They instantly fix colour, shape, minor gaps, and chips. The "celebrity smile" treatment.

**Components of a typical makeover:**

1. Professional cleaning and whitening for a bright base
2. Veneers or bonding to perfect shape and symmetry
3. Gum contouring if needed for balanced proportions
4. Replacement of any missing teeth

## The Smile Design Process

1. **Consultation** — tell us what you'd like to change; we photograph and analyse your smile
2. **Smile design plan** — we map out treatments, timeline, and cost
3. **Preview** — for veneer cases, a mock-up lets you see your new smile before committing
4. **Treatment phases** — completed over days to weeks depending on the plan
5. **The reveal** — your new smile!

## How Long Does It Take?

A whitening + bonding makeover can finish in **a single week**. Cases involving alignment take longer. Many patients plan makeovers before weddings — ideally start 3–6 months ahead for alignment cases, or 2–3 weeks ahead for veneers and whitening.

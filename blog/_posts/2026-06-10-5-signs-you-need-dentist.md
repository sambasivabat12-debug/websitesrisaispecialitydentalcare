## Why Dental Warning Signs Should Never Be Ignored

Most people visit a dentist only when the pain becomes unbearable. But by that time, a simple cavity may have turned into a root canal — or worse. As dentists with over 35 years of experience in Hyderabad, we've seen patients delay treatment until small problems became costly procedures.

Here are **5 warning signs** you should never ignore.

---

## 1. Persistent Toothache or Sensitivity

A toothache that lasts more than 2 days is your body telling you something is wrong. Whether it's temperature sensitivity (pain when drinking hot chai or cold water) or a constant dull ache — these are early signs of decay or infection.

**What we do:** At Sri Sai Speciality Dental Care, we use digital X-rays to identify the exact cause and treat it before it spreads.

---

## 2. Bleeding Gums When You Brush

Occasional bleeding can happen if you brush too hard. But **regular bleeding** — especially without pressure — is an early sign of **gum disease (gingivitis or periodontitis)**.

> Gum disease affects nearly 50% of adults over 30. The good news: caught early, it's completely reversible.

**What to do:** Schedule a scaling and cleaning appointment. Do not ignore it.

---

## 3. Loose or Shifting Teeth

Adult teeth should never feel loose. If you notice teeth moving, gaps widening, or a change in your bite — this could mean advanced gum disease or bone loss.

---

## 4. Mouth Sores That Don't Heal

Most mouth ulcers heal in 1–2 weeks. If a sore lasts **longer than 3 weeks**, doesn't heal, or keeps growing — get it checked immediately. In rare cases this can be an early sign of oral cancer, which is highly treatable when caught early.

---

## 5. Jaw Pain or Clicking Sound

Pain or clicking in the jaw joint (TMJ) when you open your mouth, chew, or yawn can indicate a TMJ disorder — common but easily managed with the right treatment.

## Why Do Teeth Turn Yellow?

Teeth lose their natural whiteness due to:

- **Tea, coffee, and cola** — the biggest culprits in India
- **Tobacco** — smoking and chewing both cause deep stains
- **Ageing** — enamel thins with age, revealing yellower dentin underneath
- **Poor brushing habits** — plaque and tartar build-up
- **Certain medications** taken during childhood

## Professional Whitening vs Home Remedies

Forget lemon, baking soda, and charcoal — these home remedies **erode your enamel** and cause permanent damage and sensitivity. Whitening toothpastes only remove surface stains slightly.

Professional in-clinic whitening uses dental-grade whitening gel activated by light, lifting stains from deep inside the enamel — safely, under expert supervision.

## What Happens During Whitening Treatment?

1. **Cleaning first** — we remove plaque and tartar so the gel works evenly
2. **Gum protection** — a protective barrier is applied to your gums
3. **Whitening gel application** — applied to teeth and activated
4. **Results in one sitting** — teeth become 2 to 6 shades lighter in about 45–60 minutes

## Is Teeth Whitening Safe?

Yes — when done by a dentist. Professional whitening does not damage enamel. Some patients feel mild sensitivity for 24–48 hours, which fades on its own. This is why whitening should never be done at unqualified salons.

## How Long Do Results Last?

Typically 1 to 3 years depending on your habits. To keep your smile bright:

- Rinse your mouth after tea or coffee
- Avoid tobacco completely
- Brush twice daily and get professional cleaning every 6 months

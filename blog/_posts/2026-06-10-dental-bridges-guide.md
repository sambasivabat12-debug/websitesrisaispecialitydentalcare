## What is a Dental Bridge?

A dental bridge literally "bridges" the gap left by one or more missing teeth. It consists of an artificial tooth held in place by crowns cemented onto the natural teeth on either side of the gap. Unlike dentures, a bridge is **fixed** — it stays in your mouth permanently.

## Why You Shouldn't Leave a Gap

A missing tooth is not just a cosmetic issue:

- Adjacent teeth **tilt and drift** into the space
- The opposing tooth **over-erupts** into the gap
- Your bite becomes uneven, straining the jaw joint
- Food lodges in the gap, causing decay in neighbouring teeth
- Chewing shifts to one side, overloading those teeth

## Types of Bridges

**Traditional bridge** — the most common. Crowns on both neighbouring teeth hold the artificial tooth between them.

**Cantilever bridge** — supported by a tooth on only one side. Used in specific situations.

**Zirconium bridge** — metal-free, exceptionally strong, and indistinguishable from natural teeth. The premium choice.

## The Bridge Procedure

1. **Preparation** — the supporting teeth are shaped under local anaesthesia
2. **Impressions** sent to the dental lab
3. **Temporary bridge** protects your teeth meanwhile
4. **Final fitting** — within 3–5 days, your permanent bridge is checked and cemented

Just two visits and your smile is complete again.

## Bridge vs Implant — Which is Better?

**Choose an implant** if the neighbouring teeth are completely healthy (no need to grind them down) and you want the longest-lasting option.

**Choose a bridge** if the neighbouring teeth already have large fillings or crowns (they benefit from being crowned anyway), you want a faster result (days, not months), or implants aren't suitable for your bone condition.

We'll examine your case honestly and recommend what's truly best for you.

## Cost of Dental Bridges in Hyderabad

Bridge cost is calculated per unit (each crown/tooth in the bridge):

- **PFM bridge:** ₹3,500 – ₹6,000 per unit
- **Zirconium bridge:** ₹8,000 – ₹15,000 per unit

A typical 3-unit bridge replaces one missing tooth.

## Caring for Your Bridge

Clean under the bridge daily using a floss threader or water flosser, brush twice daily, and visit for regular check-ups. A well-maintained bridge lasts 10–15+ years.

## Bad Breath is a Symptom, Not a Condition

Chronic bad breath (halitosis) affects 1 in 4 people, harming confidence, relationships, and careers. Mints, sprays, and chewing gum only mask the smell for minutes. To eliminate bad breath permanently, you must treat its **source** — and 90% of the time, that source is inside the mouth.

## The Real Causes of Bad Breath

**1. Tongue coating** — the #1 cause. The back of the tongue harbours bacteria that produce sulphur compounds (the rotten-egg smell). If you've never cleaned your tongue, this is almost certainly your problem.

**2. Gum disease** — infected gum pockets are bacteria factories producing a persistent, distinctive odour. If your gums bleed, your breath suffers.

**3. Food trapped between teeth** — decaying food particles between teeth that brushing can't reach. Daily flossing solves it.

**4. Deep cavities** — decayed tooth structure traps rotting debris.

**5. Dry mouth** — saliva naturally cleanses the mouth. Mouth-breathing, dehydration, and certain medications reduce saliva. This is why "morning breath" happens.

**6. Tobacco and alcohol** — both cause direct odour and dry the mouth.

**7. Non-dental causes (10%)** — sinusitis, tonsil stones, acid reflux, and diabetes. If your mouth is healthy and breath odour persists, we'll guide you to the right specialist.

## How We Diagnose and Treat Bad Breath

1. **Complete oral examination** — checking gums, cavities, tongue, and saliva
2. **Professional cleaning** — removing tartar and bacteria deposits above and below the gum line
3. **Treating the cause** — filling cavities, treating gum pockets
4. **Personalised home routine** to keep breath fresh permanently

Most patients notice a dramatic difference within days of professional cleaning.

## Your Daily Fresh Breath Routine

- **Clean your tongue daily** with a tongue scraper — back to front, 3–4 strokes
- Brush twice daily, floss once daily
- Drink plenty of water through the day
- Eat fibrous foods (apple, carrot, cucumber) that naturally cleanse
- Avoid prolonged empty stomach — fasting breath is real
- Limit raw onion/garlic before meetings (their compounds enter your lungs through the bloodstream — they exhale for hours)

## The Self-Test

Lick the inside of your wrist, let it dry 10 seconds, then smell. That's roughly how your breath smells to others. If it fails the test — book a cleaning.

## What Are Dental Implants?

A dental implant is a small titanium screw placed into your jawbone that acts as an artificial tooth root. A natural-looking crown is fixed on top, giving you a replacement tooth that looks, feels, and functions exactly like a real one.

Implants are the **gold standard** for replacing missing teeth — they can last a lifetime with proper care.

## Why Replace Missing Teeth?

Many people ignore a missing tooth, especially a back tooth. This is a mistake:

- Neighbouring teeth **shift into the gap**, disturbing your bite
- The jawbone in that area **starts shrinking** within months
- Chewing pressure overloads other teeth, causing them to wear or crack
- Facial muscles can sag, making you look older

## Implant vs Bridge vs Denture

**Dental implant** — permanent, doesn't touch neighbouring teeth, prevents bone loss, lasts 20+ years.

**Bridge** — requires grinding down two healthy neighbouring teeth, lasts 10–15 years.

**Removable denture** — least expensive but moves while eating, needs daily removal, accelerates bone loss.

If your jawbone is healthy, an implant is almost always the best long-term investment.

## The Implant Procedure

1. **Consultation and X-ray/CBCT scan** to check bone quality
2. **Implant placement** — a minor procedure under local anaesthesia, takes 30–60 minutes
3. **Healing period** — 3 to 6 months while the implant fuses with bone (osseointegration)
4. **Crown placement** — your permanent new tooth is fixed on top

Most patients return to normal work the next day.

## Does Getting an Implant Hurt?

The procedure is done under local anaesthesia — you feel pressure but no pain. Most patients say it's easier than a tooth extraction. Mild soreness for 2–3 days is managed with simple painkillers.

## Cost of Dental Implants in Hyderabad

Implant cost depends on the brand and bone condition:

- **Standard implant with crown:** ₹25,000 – ₹40,000
- **Premium implant systems:** ₹40,000 – ₹60,000

This is a one-time investment that can last decades — often cheaper than replacing bridges or dentures multiple times.

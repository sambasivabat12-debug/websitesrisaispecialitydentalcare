# 📸 Blog Images — Download Guide

**Goal:** One unique, real photo for each of your 21 blog articles.

Every link below is pre-filtered to the right topic on **Unsplash** (✓ free for commercial use, ✓ no attribution, ✓ copyright-free). Just open the link, pick the photo you like best, download it, **rename it to the exact filename shown**, and drop it in the **`blog/img/`** folder.

---

## How to do it (per image)

1. Click the **Browse link** for an article.
2. Pick a photo you like → click it → click the **"Download free"** button.
   - If there's a small ▾ arrow next to Download, choose **"Small"** (640px) — plenty sharp for a blog and keeps your site fast.
3. **Rename** the downloaded file to the exact **Save as** name (keep it lowercase, keep the `.jpg`).
4. Put it in the **`blog/img/`** folder of your project.

When all 21 are in `blog/img/`, either **upload them to GitHub** (drag-drop into the `blog/img` folder on github.com) **or send them to me** and I'll commit + wire every article to its photo in one go.

## Tips for the best result

- **Landscape (horizontal) photos** look best — they fill the article banner and card cleanly.
- Keep each file **under ~300 KB** (use the "Small" download size, or send them to me and I'll compress).
- Want a different vibe for any article? Just browse a related search (e.g. swap `toothache` for `dental-pain`) — any photo works as long as you save it with the right filename.
- You don't have to do all 21 at once. Any article whose photo isn't added yet keeps its current illustration (the fallback is built in), so the site never shows a broken image.

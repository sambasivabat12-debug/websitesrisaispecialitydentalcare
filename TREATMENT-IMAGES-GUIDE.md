- Save photos as **`.webp`** (smaller/faster), exact lowercase filenames below.

- **Your current photos stay in place until you upload a replacement** — so you can do this gradually with zero broken images.

## Tips

- **Landscape (horizontal) photos** work best for these slots.
- Keep each file **under ~300 KB** (use Unsplash's "Small" download size) so pages stay fast.
- Every link is pre-filtered on **Unsplash** (free for commercial use, no attribution needed) — pick whichever photo you like, just keep the filename.
- Don't have a perfect match for one slot? Skip it — the current photo stays until you upload.
- **Easiest path:** download what you want, then send the files to me here and I'll drop them into `treatments-img/` and they go live.

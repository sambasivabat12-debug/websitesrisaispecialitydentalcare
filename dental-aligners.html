What Are Invisible Aligners

Orthodontics That Works Around You

Invisible Aligners are clear, custom-made orthodontic trays designed to gradually move your teeth into their ideal position. Unlike traditional metal braces, aligners are virtually invisible, removable, and comfortable.

Using advanced 3D digital scanning and treatment-planning software, we map out every step of your smile transformation before treatment even begins — so you can visualize your results from day one.

FAQs

Common Questions Answered

Who is a good candidate for Invisible Aligners?
+
Most teenagers and adults with mild to moderate crowding, spacing, or bite issues are excellent candidates. Our dentists will assess your specific case during a consultation.
How long does aligner treatment take?
+
Most treatments are completed within 6–18 months, depending on the complexity of your case. Many mild cases finish in under a year.
How many hours per day should I wear my aligners?
+
For the best results, aligners should be worn 20–22 hours per day. You remove them only for eating, drinking (anything other than water), brushing, and flossing.
Can I eat while wearing my aligners?
+
No. Aligners must be removed before eating or drinking anything other than plain water. This protects the aligners from damage and staining, and keeps your teeth clean.
Are Invisible Aligners painful?
+
You may experience mild pressure for the first day or two when switching to a new aligner tray. This is a sign the aligners are working. Discomfort is generally much less than with traditional braces.
How do I clean my aligners?
+
Rinse and clean your aligners daily using a soft toothbrush and lukewarm water. Avoid hot water as it can distort the plastic. Aligner cleaning crystals are also available.
Will I need retainers after treatment?
+
Yes. Retainers are essential after completing aligner treatment. They maintain your new smile and prevent teeth from slowly shifting back over time. We'll provide custom-fitted retainers at completion.
